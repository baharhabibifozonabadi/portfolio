Title for Portfolio:
“Consultant in Theater Performance on Abortion”

Short Description for Portfolio:
In this production, with parental consent, I collaborated with the director to explore the topic of abortion through the lenses of Lacan and Freud. My role focused on research, dramaturgy, and supporting the creative and conceptual development of the work.

3 
Title for Portfolio (Option 2):
“Kazakh-Khaneh: Exploring Space, Conflict, and Memory”

Short Description for Portfolio:
A video-art documentary-performance created at the site of my undergraduate university, located in the historic Kazakh-Khaneh building, formerly housing Iranian soldiers. This work studies the university and conflict-marked environments, exploring spatial and temporal simultaneity through Lacanian and Foucauldian concepts. It serves as a tribute, connecting the lives of soldiers and students, and reflecting on shared histories within the same space.

4
Title for Portfolio:
“Playwright and Director: Alef Ayn Dām”

Short Description for Portfolio:
An experimental theater project exploring language stripped of biological context through the interactions of two isolated characters. The work applies linguistic and cognitive analysis to theatrical practice, investigating how meaning and communication emerge in constrained and abstract settings.

5
Title for Portfolio:
“Playwright and Dramaturge: Incendies (Thesis Project)”

Short Description for Portfolio:
A dramaturgical project combining psychoanalytic analysis with political-mythological modeling. The work employs a polyphonic structure to reconstruct historically and spatially coherent spaces—ranging from utopia and heterotopia to dystopia, across prehistoric, historical, and modern contexts.

**Title for Portfolio:**
*“Playwright and Director:mother of blood (om al dam) 

**Short Description for Portfolio:**
Selected for the RooBana Inter-University Event, this project examines language as a tool incapable of fulfilling desire, reflecting a fragmented identity and a political body. The work situates these explorations within the historical and cultural context of the Qajar period.

7 
Title for Portfolio:
“Story: Fosuf Yaqtooluka Yaqinan (فسوف یقتلک یقینا)”

Short Description for Portfolio:
This narrative explores identity fragmentation and memory loss caused by trauma. It follows a man or woman living in an unfamiliar land, isolated and driven to madness. Their connections with people from their past life have faded, leading to a profound existential crisis where even basic perceptions, such as whether the weather is hot, become impossible to determine. The story examines how displacement and cultural alienation destabilize the self.
